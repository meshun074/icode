Library Donations Feature Instructions

Library Home Donations Feature Home Hints

Overview

Welcome to the Library Donate Feature! This feature allows users to make donations to support our library. The donations help us acquire new books, improve our facilities, and provide better services to our patrons. We appreciate all the generosity and support in helping us fulfill our mission of promoting literacy and knowledge.


As we well know, time is money. And, every minute our site is down may result in irreparable damage to our annual budget. So, please hurry and help us fix the bugs.

Transperancy is the life blood of non-profits. Having our budgetary allocations questioned won't go over well. So, your first task is to fix the bugs in the "AllDonations" page.
