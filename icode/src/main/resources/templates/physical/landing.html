Physical Therapy

Welcome to the Physical Therapy Center. This application will assist physical therapists and their clients as they work through the recovery process. For Physical therapists, they will be able to add new clients, search through available exercises, assign clients workout assignments (collections of exercises), and update their contact information. For physical therapy clients, this application will allow them access sample videos of their workouts, and mark workouts as done when they are completed, view their physical therapists information and update their own contact information.

Assignments

You are on the team that is building the Physical therapists' ability to create assignments functionality for the current application. You will be building out how assignments are created, assigned to clients, and viewed by clients. Below you are provided with the business requirements for this component of the application. There is a collection of available clients, exercises and their data included the icode/physical/data package of the iCode application.

Tasks

  • Create functionality
    • Therapists should have the functionality to create new assignments, indicating the total number of times it should be completed
    • Therapists will be able to search through the current exercises add them to the assignment: specifying the reps and sets of the workout
    • Therapists will be able to associate the assignment with a client
  • Cliet Interaction
    • Clients will be able to view a list of all their assignments
    • Clients will be able to view assignment details: checkbox list of workouts displaying exercise name, reps, and sets
    • Clients will be able to view exercise details
    • Clients will be able to mark workouts as completed, this will reflect on the UI as strike through or disabled checkbox

    Additional tasks

  • Clients will be able to reset their assignments (incrementing times completing, reseting workouts to not completed)
  • List of assignments can display different colors indicating whether an assignment is completed, in progress, and not started
  • Create unique styles for your HTML pages
